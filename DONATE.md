## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**  
A: VitaGraph is currently operating as an independent research project. Tax deductibility depends on your jurisdiction. We recommend consulting a tax advisor. We can provide donation receipts for your records.

**Q: Can I donate without revealing my identity?**  
A: Yes! Cryptocurrency donations are pseudonymous. If using bank transfers, your bank knows your identity but we only see the transfer. Your identity is never published publicly without your explicit consent.

**Q: What currency should I use?**  
A: Use whatever is most convenient. Crypto is borderless and instant. Bank transfers can be in EUR, GBP, USD, or RON—we'll handle any currency conversion on Wise's favorable rates.

**Q: Can I request a specific research focus?**  
A: Absolutely! Email or open a GitHub Issue to discuss how your donation can be directed. We welcome input from our supporters.

**Q: Is there a minimum donation?**  
A: No minimum! Donations of any size are meaningful and appreciated. Even €5 contributes.

**Q: Can I set up a recurring donation?**  
A: Yes, Wise allows recurring transfers. Or use GitHub Sponsors (coming soon).
